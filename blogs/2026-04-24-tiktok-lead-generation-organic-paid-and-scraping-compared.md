<p>Facebook is cheaper for legal and real estate, but TikTok reaches 18-34 year-olds who are harder to find on Facebook.</p><h3 id="organic">Organic</h3><p>TikTok Business Accounts can collect leads without ad spend through <a href="https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/business-account-lead-generation?lang=en">in-app Instant Forms</a> attached to a <strong>CTA</strong> button on the profile, a <strong>Video Anchor</strong> overlay on organic videos, or a <strong>Live Anchor</strong> overlay during livestreams. All three require 1,000 followers or Business Registration. The cap is 420 leads per week per account, combined across placements.</p><p>Business Accounts also include <a href="https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/navigate-auto-message-business-accounts?lang=en">native DM automation</a>: welcome messages, keyword replies, suggested questions, and chat prompts. The comment-to-DM workflow is to say &quot;DM me [keyword]&quot; in your video - the viewer sends the keyword and TikTok auto-replies with your lead magnet.</p><p>The downside is that a Business Account restricts your music library to the Commercial Music Library, which removes access to the trending sounds that help organic reach.</p><p>Both paid ads and organic require you to be the content creator - you need videos, an audience, or an ad budget. <strong>Scraping lets you prospect from other creators&apos; audiences instead.</strong></p><h2 id="building-prospect-lists-from-tiktok-with-apify">Building prospect lists from TikTok with Apify</h2><p>You can scrape TikTok comment and profile data to build prospect lists for cold outreach.
